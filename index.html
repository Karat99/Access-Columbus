Access Columbus Mobile App


In our effort to ensure inclusivity, we have made our Accessibility Guide into a brand new app. This app includes more attractions, the ability to save favorites, and attraction reviews from fellow users. We have gone above and beyond to make this app for tourists and Columbus residents alike so we hope you will enjoy it. You can see images of the app below.
